================================================================================
                    🆘 SOMETHING NOT WORKING?
================================================================================

   ❌ PROBLEM: Website shows "Server not connected"
   ✅ FIX:     Go back to Step 1 and start the server again

   ❌ PROBLEM: Double-clicking START_SERVER.bat does nothing
   ✅ FIX:     Right-click it → "Run as administrator"

   ❌ PROBLEM: Browser shows blank page
   ✅ FIX:     Press F5 to refresh the page

   ❌ PROBLEM: Takes too long (more than 2 minutes)
   ✅ FIX:     Close everything and start from Step 1 again

                    📞 STILL NEED HELP?
================================================================================

   1. Check the status bar at the bottom of the website
      🟢 Green = Everything is working

   2. Look at the black window - any red error messages?

   3. Try closing everything and starting fresh from Step 1
